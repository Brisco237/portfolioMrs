The ARTist


Rafael Paiva has been a Professional Photographer and Photo Retoucher since 2010.

His rich portfolio is marked by collaborations with a wide spectrum of clients, ranging from major corporations to independent artists. His specialties are Commercial Photography and Personal Branding content.

With a career steeped in both creativity and technical expertise, Rafael has emerged as a well-round professional known for his ability to navigate the unique demands of a diverse high-end clientele.

Rafael's corporate experience is strong and on-going. He worked as Senior Retoucher for Nike/Converse from 2018/2024, where his mastery of detail and innovative vision significantly contributed to the brand's visual.

He currently works at Apple TV as Senior Retoucher for Sports and TV.

Rafael started to expand beyond photography in 2024. His explorations around Filmmaking reflects his passion for pushing the boundaries of visual storytelling.

Rafael's journey underscores not only his adaptability but also his pivotal role in shaping and enhancing the visual identities of the clients he works.
